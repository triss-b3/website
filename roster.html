              0. Square machines and declined sales 
             During a card sale, please take a few extra seconds
             to confirm the payment goes through successfully. It's
             more troublesome to run after people and get them to
             come back. It'll also teach customers to wait until the
             card company approves the sale, before they head off.
             If square doesn't say "confirmed", but the customer's
             phone says "paid", this means Square didn't process it
             (and the phone confirmed too early). However we can't do
             anything about this besides telling customer "Please
             check later and if you weren't charged, please come back"

              5. Taking food for yourself 
             Guys a couple of people have asked me how much food
             each person can take for themselves each day, for eg break
             time. To clear up any confusion, the answer is -
             - 1 baguette or 1 croissant
             - 1 bottle of water
             Any extra food or drink you want to take, please refer to
             above "employee price list" and purchase them at that
             price.

              6. When you arrive at work 
             When you get to work, you should be ready to get straight
             to it. If you need to do your hair or makeup, this should
             be done  before  you arrive to work. We have noticed
             a few people taking too long to get ready when they arrive,
             so please double check your morning preparations and if
             you're taking longer than a minute or two to get ready
             once you arrive, then you should wake up earlier.

             7. When you're late to work

             time to arrive is 07:30.
